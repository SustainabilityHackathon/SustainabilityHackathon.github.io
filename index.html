Rules

  1. Your projects must be original, they cannot be copied and pasted from another source.
  2. All competitors must complete team formation by the 23rd at 11:30 AM.
  3. All teams must attend at least three mentor reviews out of four.
  4. The deadline for submitting the final presentation video is September 26th 10:00 AM
  5. At the time of presentation, all teams must be presented
  6. Winners receive cash prizes in the form of Amazon gift cards.
  7. Only the team that has presented will receive participation certificates.
  8. During the hackathon, all communications are conducted viaWhatsapp Group.
